GPS GPS Site Camera: Stamp

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how GPS Site Camera: Stamp handles information when you use the mobile application.

Effective date: July 20, 2026

Privacy at a glance Photos and videos stay on your device by default. The app uses foreground location for GPS stamps and may send coordinates to mapping or geocoding services to provide map and address features. We do not sell your personal information.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to GPS Site Camera: Stamp, including the Android application identified by the package name com.geostamp.gpscamera and the corresponding iOS application (the “App”). The App is provided by Vishal Bhimporwala (“we,” “us,” or “our”).

By using the App, you acknowledge the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, do not use the App or grant permissions that you do not want the App to access.

2. Information the App handles

The App may access or process the following information only as needed for its camera and site-documentation features:

Camera and media

Photos, videos, thumbnails, PDF reports, and associated capture metadata created through the App.

Precise or approximate location

Latitude, longitude, accuracy, altitude, provider, date, time, and an address derived from the location.

Audio

Microphone audio only when you record a video with voice enabled.

App settings

Language, onboarding state, camera preferences, stamp templates, custom stamp text, and related local settings.

Purchase information

If paid products are offered, Google Play may process product, transaction, subscription, and purchase-status information.

Technical request information

Mapping providers may receive an IP address, request metadata, and coordinates when map or address features are used.

The App does not currently require an account, and we do not operate an application server that receives your captured photos, videos, or location history. The current version does not include a third-party advertising SDK or third-party analytics SDK.

3. How information is used

  • To provide the live camera preview and capture photos or videos.
  • To add coordinates, address, date, time, accuracy, altitude, custom text, maps, and related details to stamps.
  • To organize captures, generate PDF reports, and let you save, export, or share content.
  • To remember your language, onboarding progress, camera controls, stamp template, and other preferences.
  • To retrieve map images and convert coordinates into a readable address.
  • To process and restore purchases if paid features are made available.
  • To diagnose failures using limited local logs that are designed not to include photos or precise location values.

4. Device permissions

You can deny or revoke permissions in your device settings. Some features will not work without the permissions required for that feature.

Permission Purpose
Camera Provides the live preview and captures site photos and videos.
Location while using the App Adds GPS coordinates and related details to captures. The App does not request background location permission.
Microphone Records sound only when video recording with voice is enabled.
Photos, media, or storage Saves exported captures to your gallery or photo library and accesses media only when you choose a supported media action. On older Android versions, legacy storage permission may be used for export.
Internet and network state Retrieves static maps, supports address lookup through platform services, and communicates with Google Play for purchases when available.

5. On-device storage and retention

Captured media and report metadata are stored in app-controlled storage on your device. Settings and cached addresses are also stored locally. Static map images may be held temporarily in memory to improve performance. Media you explicitly export is copied to your device gallery, photo library, or another destination you select.

App-controlled media and settings generally remain until you delete them, clear the App’s data, or uninstall the App. Exported copies remain in the destination you selected until you delete them there. We do not control retention by your device provider, backup service, sharing destination, or third-party service.

6. Sharing and third-party processing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Information may leave your device in these limited circumstances:

  • Google Maps Platform: When map thumbnails are enabled, coordinates are sent to Google Maps Platform to retrieve a static map. Google may also receive normal network request information. See the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Platform geocoding services: Android or Apple location services may process coordinates to return a readable address. Their handling is governed by the privacy terms of Google, Apple, your device manufacturer, or the configured service provider.
  • Google Play Billing: If you make a purchase, Google Play processes payment and transaction information under Google’s policies. We do not receive your full payment card number.
  • User-directed sharing: When you use Save, Export, or Share, the selected photo, video, or report is provided to the gallery, photo library, application, service, or person you choose. The recipient’s privacy practices then apply.
  • Legal and safety reasons: We may disclose information if required by applicable law or if reasonably necessary to protect users, rights, safety, or the integrity of the App. Because we do not operate a media-storage server, we generally do not possess your captures.

7. Your choices and deletion

  • Use device settings to review or revoke camera, location, microphone, and photo-library permissions.
  • Disable map thumbnails in the App settings to avoid Static Maps requests for stamp previews.
  • Delete captures using the App’s Collection controls where available.
  • Delete exported copies from your gallery, photo library, file manager, or sharing destination.
  • Clear the App’s storage or uninstall the App to remove app-controlled local data.
  • Contact us using the email below for privacy questions. Because the App does not currently use accounts or store captures on our servers, we may have no server-side personal record to identify or delete.

8. Security

We use platform application storage, permission controls, scoped sharing mechanisms, and encrypted HTTPS connections for Static Maps requests. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for protecting access to your device and reviewing sensitive location details before sharing stamped media.

9. Children’s privacy

The App is a general-purpose site documentation tool and is not directed to children under 13 or the minimum age required by local law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through an account or developer-operated server. If you believe a child has provided personal information directly to us, contact us so we can address the request.

10. International processing

Third-party providers such as Google or Apple may process request information in countries other than your own. Their privacy policies describe their international data-handling practices and safeguards.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the App’s features, third-party services, or legal obligations change. The updated version will be posted at the same public URL with a revised effective date. Material changes may also be communicated in the App or store listing where appropriate.
