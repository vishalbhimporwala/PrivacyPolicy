DrivePulse Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026

App name: DrivePulse

Package name: com.drivepulse.gps

DrivePulse is a GPS driving utility app that provides live map viewing, GPS speed display, trip recording, trip history, and map measurement tools. This Privacy Policy explains what information DrivePulse uses, how it is stored, and how users can control it.

DrivePulse is designed to work with local on-device storage. The current version does not require account login and does not sync your trip or measurement data to a DrivePulse cloud server.

Information We Collect Or Use

DrivePulse may use the following information when you use the app:

  • Approximate location: Used to show your general position on the map when location permission is granted.
  • Precise location: Used to show current location, calculate live speed, record trip route points while the app is open, and support map measurement features.
  • Trip data: Includes trip duration, distance, speed statistics, and route points created when you start and stop trip recording.
  • Measurement data: Includes saved distance, area, and radius measurements, titles, units, and selected map points.
  • App settings and map preferences: Such as selected map type, dark mode, and selected units where applicable.

How We Use Information

DrivePulse uses information only to provide app features, including:

  • Showing your current position on the map.
  • Displaying live GPS speed.
  • Recording trips while the app is open.
  • Showing trip history and trip detail screens.
  • Creating, saving, editing, and deleting map measurements.
  • Improving accuracy of distance, area, radius, and route calculations.

Location Permission

DrivePulse requests location permission because core features depend on location, including current location on the map, live speed calculation, trip recording, and map-based measurement workflows.

You can deny location permission and still use map viewing and measurement features manually. Features that require current location or GPS speed may not work until location permission and device location services are enabled.

The current closed-testing version does not declare Android background location permission. Trip recording is intended for use while the app is open.

Local Storage

Saved trips and saved measurements are stored locally on your device using the app database. DrivePulse does not currently upload this data to a DrivePulse server.

Android app data backup is disabled for this version so local app data is not backed up by Android's automatic app backup mechanism.

Data Sharing

DrivePulse does not sell your personal data. DrivePulse does not currently share your saved trip or measurement records with a DrivePulse backend or cloud sync service.

DrivePulse uses Google Maps services to display maps and traffic-related map data. Google Maps may process technical and location-related data according to Google's own terms and privacy policies. You can review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Deletion

You can delete saved measurements from the Saved Measurements screen. You can delete saved trips from the Trip History screen. You may also delete all local DrivePulse app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage from your device settings.

Children's Privacy

DrivePulse is not directed to children under 13. The app is intended for driving, travel, and map utility use. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the issue.

Security

DrivePulse stores app-created trip and measurement data locally on your device. No method of electronic storage is completely secure, but the app is designed to avoid unnecessary cloud transfer of your saved trip and measurement records in the current version.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when DrivePulse adds new features, such as cloud sync, exports, analytics, premium purchases, ads, or background trip recording. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
